About

Welcome to Imperial College Cycling Club – home to all cycling activities at Imperial!

Who we are

We're a student-run club for road cyclists and mountain bikers of all levels. Whether you're into fast-paced training rides, casual Sunday spins, or exploring muddy singletrack, ICC is your home on two wheels. We ride, race, chill, and support each other on and off the bike.

From weekly club rides to weekend adventures, we're here to build skills, make friends, and enjoy the ride – together.
