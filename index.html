About the Club

Imperial Cycling Club is a student-run society open to all levels and disciplines. We're here to build community, ride together, and make the most of cycling in and beyond London. Supported by the Student Union, we welcome everyone – from complete beginners to seasoned racers.

Learn more

Road Biking

Join us for weekly group rides, tempo sessions, or weekend escapes. Whether you're commuting, training, or racing BUCS – you'll find fellow road cyclists at ICC. We roll out together and support each other on the way.

Learn more

MTB & Adventure

From local trail sessions in Richmond Park to full-day rides at Swinley or BikePark Wales – ICC MTB is all about mud, flow, and fun. No pressure, just good rides with good people.
